The Athena Program:

Athena-1: Tests launch scripts to a target's inclination.
Athena-2: Tests establishing a transfer orbit, assuming an established parking orbit.
Athena-3: Tests comms library.
Athena-4: Tests launch to target plane, burn to transfer orbit, capture and adjust for landing.
Athena-5: Expands Athena 4 to zero inclination at target orbit.
Athena-6: Expands Athena 5 to test spot landing script.
Athena-7: First manned Athena mission to Minmus!
